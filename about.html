About

Hello welcome to Rock'n Dough Pizzeria and Brewer's website. Serve Great food in an great enviornment for anyone any age to enjoy. We offer a variety of drinks, food choices and more. We hope to see you soon in our Restruant.

How We Began

We began as a Food Truck, in Memphis,TN and serving pizza from a wood fired oven. After a year of success, Rock'n Dough opened a location in Memphis. After a few months Rock'n Dough also opened a restraunt in Downtown Jackson,TN. We are continuing to grow our business as we are soon opening a restraunt in Germantown,TN.
